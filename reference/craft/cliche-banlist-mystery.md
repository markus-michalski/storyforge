# Cliché Banlist — Mystery Genre Override

Extends `cliche-banlist.md` with mystery/detective-specific entries.

## Detective Convention
- the game is afoot (severity: high)
- elementary (severity: high)
- it was the butler (severity: high)
- I had a hunch (severity: medium)
- something didn't add up (severity: medium)
- the pieces were falling into place (severity: high)
- all the pieces clicked together (severity: high)
- it all made sense now (severity: high)
- the truth was staring him in the face (severity: high)
- the truth was staring her in the face (severity: high)
- the answer had been there all along (severity: high)
- hiding in plain sight (severity: high)

## Red Herring Tells
- too obvious to be the killer (severity: high) — tells the reader to dismiss
- clearly the most suspicious (severity: medium)
- he had means, motive, and opportunity (severity: medium) — as clincher
- she had means, motive, and opportunity (severity: medium)
- the perfect crime (severity: high)
- no one would suspect (severity: high)
- left no trace (severity: medium)

## Crime Scene
- the body was found (severity: medium) — as chapter opener
- the victim lay (severity: medium)
- there was no sign of forced entry (severity: medium)
- time of death (severity: medium) — as formulaic exposition
- blunt force trauma (severity: medium)
- she had been dead for hours (severity: medium)
- he had been dead for hours (severity: medium)

## Interrogation
- where were you the night of (severity: high)
- I know you did it (severity: medium)
- you're the only one who could have (severity: medium)
- why don't you tell me what really happened (severity: medium)
- I think you know more than you're letting on (severity: medium)
- you have the right to remain silent (severity: medium) — when used dramatically out of context

## Climax Reveal
- you see (severity: high) — villain-explains-everything opener
- it all started when (severity: high) — reveal monologue
- I would have gotten away with it (severity: high)
- and that's when I knew (severity: medium)
- the killer was in this room all along (severity: high)
- it was you all along (severity: high)

## Amateur Detective
- she had a nose for trouble (severity: high)
- he had a nose for trouble (severity: high)
- she couldn't help herself (severity: medium) — compulsive snooping
- something about it nagged at her (severity: medium)
- something about it nagged at him (severity: medium)
- her curiosity got the better of her (severity: medium)
- his curiosity got the better of him (severity: medium)
