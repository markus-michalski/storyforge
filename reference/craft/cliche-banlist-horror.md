# Cliché Banlist — Horror Genre Override

Extends `cliche-banlist.md` with horror-specific entries.

## Atmosphere / Setting
- it was a dark and stormy night (severity: high) — already in base, reinforced
- the house seemed to breathe (severity: medium)
- the walls seemed to close in (severity: high)
- the darkness pressed in (severity: medium)
- the shadows seemed to move (severity: medium)
- something ancient and evil (severity: high)
- malevolent presence (severity: medium)
- the air grew heavy (severity: medium)
- an unnatural chill (severity: medium)
- the temperature dropped (severity: medium) — as horror signal
- the lights flickered (severity: high)
- the candle went out (severity: medium)

## Fear Response
- too afraid to scream (severity: high)
- frozen with fear (severity: high)
- paralyzed by fear (severity: high)
- fear gripped him (severity: high)
- fear gripped her (severity: high)
- dread washed over him (severity: high)
- dread washed over her (severity: high)
- he couldn't move (severity: medium) — as jump-scare beat
- she couldn't move (severity: medium)
- his blood turned to ice (severity: high)
- her blood turned to ice (severity: high)
- he couldn't breathe (severity: medium) — as sustained horror
- she couldn't breathe (severity: medium)

## Monster / Creature Description
- red eyes gleamed in the darkness (severity: high)
- eyes that were not human (severity: high)
- a smile that didn't reach its eyes (severity: medium)
- it shouldn't be alive (severity: high)
- impossible angles (severity: medium) — overused cosmic horror
- too many teeth (severity: medium)
- moving wrong (severity: medium) — when used as sole descriptor
- it was ancient (severity: medium) — as sole characterisation
- older than time (severity: high)
- older than humanity (severity: high)

## Gore / Violence
- a spray of blood (severity: medium)
- blood everywhere (severity: high) — use specifics instead
- he barely felt the pain (severity: medium)
- she barely felt the pain (severity: medium)
- the world went red (severity: high)
- merciful unconsciousness (severity: high)

## Supernatural Tells
- evil incarnate (severity: high)
- pure evil (severity: high)
- a force beyond understanding (severity: medium)
- something from beyond (severity: medium)
- it defied explanation (severity: medium)
- the laws of nature did not apply (severity: medium)

## Jump Scare / Reveal
- it was just the cat (severity: high)
- it was just the wind (severity: high)
- but then she turned around (severity: medium) — as jump-scare setup
- standing right behind him (severity: medium)
- standing right behind her (severity: medium)
- it was already too late (severity: medium)
