# Cliché Banlist — Sci-Fi Genre Override

Extends `cliche-banlist.md` with science-fiction-specific entries.

## Tech-Exposition (As-You-Know-Bob)
- as you know (severity: high) — worst offender in SF exposition
- as you are aware (severity: high)
- the computer said (severity: medium) — when used as exposition dump
- the AI responded (severity: medium) — as exposition device
- according to my calculations (severity: high)
- sensors indicate (severity: medium) — overused bridge cliché
- Captain, I'm detecting (severity: high)
- analysis complete (severity: medium)
