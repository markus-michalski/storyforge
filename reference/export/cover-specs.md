**Universal safe size:** 2560 x 3840 px at 300 DPI (works everywhere after resizing).

## Design Rules

### Typography on Covers
- **Title:** Largest element. Must be readable at thumbnail size (50px wide).
- **Author name:** Smaller than title. Consistent across series.
- **Tagline:** Optional. Short (max 8 words). Below title or above author.
- **Font:** Match genre expectations:
  - Horror: distressed, dripping, gothic
  - Fantasy: ornate serif, medieval
  - Thriller: bold sans-serif, clean
  - Romance: elegant script + serif
  - Sci-Fi: geometric sans, futuristic
  - Literary: refined serif, minimal

### Composition
- **One focal point.** Don't overcrowd.
- **Rule of thirds.** Place key elements on intersection points.
- **Negative space.** Leave room for title/author text.
- **Color psychology:**
  - Red: passion, danger, power
  - Blue: trust, mystery, cold
  - Black: dark, elegant, horror
  - Gold: premium, historical, fantasy
  - White: clean, literary, minimalist
  - Purple: supernatural, royalty, mystery

### The Thumbnail Test
Your cover must work at 160x256 pixels. If you can't read the title at this size, redesign. Most book discovery happens via thumbnails on phone screens.

## AI Art Prompt Structure

### For Midjourney
```
/imagine [subject] [action/pose] [setting/background],
[art style], [lighting], [mood/atmosphere],
[color palette], book cover composition,
professional book cover art, no text,
--ar 2:3 --stylize 500 --v 6
```

### For DALL-E
```
A professional book cover illustration for a [genre] novel.
[Main visual element described in detail].
[Background/setting]. [Lighting description].
[Mood and atmosphere]. [Color palette].
Style: [photorealistic/digital painting/illustration/graphic].
Vertical composition (2:3 ratio). No text or lettering.
```

### Style Keywords by Genre
- **Horror:** dark, atmospheric, ominous, shadowy, isolated, decay, fog
- **Fantasy:** epic, sweeping, magical, luminous, ancient, mystical
- **Sci-Fi:** sleek, neon, vast, technological, futuristic, cosmic
- **Thriller:** stark, high contrast, urban, tense, surveillance
- **Romance:** warm, soft focus, intimate, golden light, emotional
- **Mystery:** noir, moody, silhouette, rain, city lights
- **Literary:** minimal, abstract, symbolic, muted, artistic

## Series Covers
- Consistent design template across all books
- Same font, layout, color scheme
- Differentiator: subtitle, number, key visual element
- Brand recognition: readers should identify the series at a glance
